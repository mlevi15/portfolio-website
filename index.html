More About Me

I was born and raised in the beautiful Okanagan Valley in British Columbia, Canada. Outside of work and school I'm an avid reader, I enjoy spending time outdoors exploring, and I'm a big fan of cinema and retro video games.

During my time at UBC studying computer and data science (2015-2020) I have had the opportunity to work on many exciting projects, both for my classes and within the community. The majority of my experience has been in web development, but I have also been involved in tech support roles and more recently I have been working on mobile application projects.
